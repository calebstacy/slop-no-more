# Onboarding copy review

In today's fast-paced world, onboarding copy matters more than ever. Most teams treat it as an afterthought. This is not about wording, it's about trust. Let's unpack this.

Research shows that users abandon confusing flows. The key insight is that every screen must earn its keep. Moreover, the copy should delve into the user's needs and foster engagement while empowering them to streamline their journey. Let that sink in.

I hope this helps!
